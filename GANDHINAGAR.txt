<H1>GUJRAT'S CAPITAL IS GANDHI NAGAR</H1>
<P>Gandhinagar, city, capital of Gujarat state, west-central India.</P>
<P> It lies on the banks of the Sabarmati River, just north of Ahmadabad.</P>
<P> Gandhinagar was named for Mohandas K. Gandhi, leader of the Indian nationalist movement. </P>
